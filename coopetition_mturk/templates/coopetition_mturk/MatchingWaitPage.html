Please wait for the system to match you with another participant...

You are being matched with another participant to continue with the experiment.

If you successfully complete the experiment, you will for sure receive $1 as your payment. Depending on your and the other participant's decision, you can also earn up to $5 as additional bonus for the rest of the experiment.

In the rare event that the system cannot find a matching for you because there are not enough participants, you are still guaranteed to be paid by $1. If you are not matched with another participant in 5 minutes, you can close the browser and leave the experiment. A $1 will be paid to you later.

During your interaction with the other participant, the following experimental interface will be shown for you to submit your decisions.

Note: The experiment proceeds as fast as the slower participant, so please submit your decisions in a prompt manner. For the first 5 rounds, you have 1 minutes to reach your decision; after Round 5, you have 30 seconds to reach your decision. If you do not submit your answer within the allocated time, the computer will randomly choose answers for you, and you will not get paid for that round. If you fail to submit your decisions too many times, you will be able to receive your experimental payment.

Remember to submit once you reach your decision. Your decision is submitted only after you click "Submit".
