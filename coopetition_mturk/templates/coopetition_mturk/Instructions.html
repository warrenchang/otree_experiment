There will be 10 rounds of decision-making.

{% if player.treatment == 'random' %} At the start of each round, you will be randomly matched with one participants. {% endif %} {% if player.treatment == 'fixed' %} At the start of this part, you are randomly matched with one participants. You will play with the same participant for all 10 rounds. {% endif %}

In each round, you receive 10 points to allocate three projects: P1, P2, and P3.

Total P1-Profit = Your P1-points × Other’s P1-points

Your share of P1-Profit = Your P2-points / (Your P2-points + Other's P2-points)

{% if player.compete == 1 %} Your earnings from P3 = 20 × Your P3-points / (Your P3-points + Other's P3-points) {% endif %} {% if player.compete == 0 %} Your earnings from P3 = Your P3-points {% endif %}

Your total earnings is determined by the following formula:

Total P1-Profit × Your share of P1-Profit + Your P3-Earnings
