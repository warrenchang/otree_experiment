Instructions

Each participant in this room is asked to choose a number between 0 and {{ Constants.guess_max }}. The winner will be the participant whose number is closest to 2/3 of the average of all chosen numbers.

The winner will receive RMB {{ Constants.jackpot }}. In case of a tie, the RMB {{ Constants.jackpot }} will be equally divided among winners.

This game will be played for {{ Constants.num_rounds }} rounds. One round will be randomly chosen for payment.

Make your decision carefully, because each round can be chosen to determine your payment.
